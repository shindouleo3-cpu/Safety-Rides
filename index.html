Purpose & Goals

Safety Ride aims to educate riders on how to protect themselves before, during, and after trips. The platform breaks down essential safety tools, driver verification steps, emergency protocols, and commuter best practices to ensure secure and informed travel.

Key Highlights

Driver Verification

Check driver identity, star ratings, plate number, and vehicle details before boarding.

Real-Time Route Monitoring

Track your trip and confirm that your driver follows the correct route.

Emergency Tools

Access in-app SOS features and quick emergency contacts during a ride.

Rider Guidelines

Learn essential safety practices and proper commuting behavior.

Share Trip Feature

Instantly share your live location and ETA with trusted contacts.

Rating System

Rate your driver after every trip to help keep the platform safe and accountable.

Your Safety Starts With Awareness

Learn practical steps to stay safe before, during, and after every ride-hailing trip.
