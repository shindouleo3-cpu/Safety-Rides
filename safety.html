Safety Tips

Before the Ride

  • Verify the Driver’s Identity: Check the driver's name, photo, vehicle model, and plate number.
  • Inspect the Vehicle: Ensure the vehicle matches the details shown in the app.
  • Check Driver Rating: Prefer drivers with consistently high ratings.
  • Share Trip Details: Share your route with someone you trust.
  • Choose Safe Pickup Points: Prefer bright, public areas.
  • Be Extra Cautious at Night: Avoid riding alone when possible.

During the Ride

  • Sit in the Back Seat: Safer and more secure, especially when alone.
  • Monitor the Route: Ensure the driver follows the correct path.
  • Protect Personal Information: Do not share private details.
  • Stay Alert: Be aware of surroundings and avoid distractions.
  • Know How to Unlock the Door: Be ready in case of emergencies.
  • Use In-App Safety Features: SOS, emergency contacts, trip reporting.
  • Trust Your Instincts: Stop the ride if something feels wrong.

After the Ride

  • Check Your Items: Make sure nothing is left behind.
  • Rate the Driver: Helps maintain safety and reliability.
  • Report Incidents: Immediately report unsafe or suspicious behavior.
  • Save Your Receipt: Useful for documentation and verification.
  • Protect Your Privacy: Avoid sharing ride details online.

General Commuter Safety Tips

  • Keep your phone fully charged.
  • Avoid showing valuables publicly.
  • Stay aware of surroundings.
  • Save emergency numbers.
  • Use well-lit and verified pickup points.
